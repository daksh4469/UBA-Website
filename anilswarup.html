Making Things Happen in the Government

Highlights of the lecture
  • Talking about the democracy of the country, he explained that it is not easy to make discussions in a country like India, which can satisfy the needs of all the citizens living with in the boundary.
  • It is essential to understand the fact that how can one implement the idea and convince the politician as well as satisfy a major part of the democracy and also ensure that no one is harmed due to the decision implemented. 
  • By sighting the case of question paper leak of CBSE class XII board examination, he explained the idea of political acceptability of a decision.
  • If one has vision, articulations, and plan of action, but the government institutions are not accepting the fact, then one has to diffuse the matter in front of the government. 
  • Telling about his experience as Director-general Labour welfare, he explained the idea of socially desirable decisions.
  • Ayushman Bharat, a scheme ideated by Dr Swarup, got world recognition as one of the most excellent and elegant schemes to date. It was paperless, cashless, as well as applicable for all those who do not have a bank account.
  • For an idea to be a success and to make it work, one need to check whether the idea is :
    • Politically acceptable
    • Socially desirable
    • Financially viable
    • Administratively feasible
    • Judicially tenable

The event was highly appreciated by the audience and received warm feedback. The audience actively interacted with Dr Anil Swarup during the event and it was a huge success.
