    GIC Chharba

    • Date: January 26, 2019

    • Village: Charbha

    • Student Participation: Over 150

    Republic day in India is celebrated every year with great pride on 26th of January to honor the Constitution of India as it came into force on the same day in the year 1950. So, the team decided to organize sports and fun activities in GIC, Chharba on this special day.

    The social benefits of outdoor sports are extremely obvious and, well, beneficial. Playing sports outdoors with a bunch of students together can improve general social development, in that students will learn to interact with each other naturally but with sports as the main social anchor of the activity. Additionally, social interaction will improve other facets such as leadership, self esteem, and general teamwork through the construct of the sports games themselves.

    This event was divided into three sessions. First session was of story writing competition, second session was of fun activities including separation anxiety, cup tower and the third session was of sport events including relays, shuttle run, dodge ball. The team and school staff honored winning students with medals, prize boxes, pens and chocolates. More than 150 students participated in this event. The main motive to organize such activities in this pride occasion is to get students motivated to compete and achieve their goal
