    IIT Roorkee Campus

    • Date: October 26, 2018

    • Village: Meerpur

    • Students Visited: 42

    Under initiatives propagated by UBA, IIT Roorkee Science Exhibitions was conducted in Junior High School, Meerpur for 8 th to 10 th standard. A great number of students showed keen interest and actively participated in the events. Some students from class 6 th and 7 th also made projects of their own. Many of them also showed a desire to pursue higher education and achieve greater heights in the future. Hence, to discover the talents hidden in bright minds, the visit was organized by the team members in a hope that this visit might bring a positive change in the lives of students.

    An outstanding example of motivation created by such visits was observed 2 years ago. It was when the same kind of visit was organized by team Chharba, UBA Meerpur in the year 2015. A student named Abhijeet Rathi was a part of that visit. He came to know that premier institutions like IITs exist and the fact that he can also be a part of such institutions in future if worked harder. And the result was that today he is a student of IIT Roorkee and also a member of Team Chharba, UBA IIT Roorkee. This shows that the imprints created on young minds last forever.

    A glimpse of IIT Roorkee campus life and the prestigious history it beholds can motivate them to work harder and achieve greater heights in future.

    The Campus Visit conducted by the team UBA, Meerpur on October 26, 2018 of 42 students (winner of science Exhibition, held on October 02, 2018) along with 2 teachers. The Campus Visit consist visit to the Library, the James Thomason Building, and the H. B. Medlicott Museum. Students were briefed about the history and legacy of IIT Roorkee, opportunity to explore the rich books collection at IIT Roorkee. Students were also given a motivational talk.
