About Winter School 2020:

  • January 12, 2020

Unnat Abhiyan Bharat, IIT Roorkee is organizing Winter school 2020 a three-day event scheduled from January 17, 2020, to January 19, 2020. It offers an unmatchable learning experience with some renowned Professors and experts to the participants in the remarkable fields of Social Entrepreneurship, Accessible India, Issues of Indian Economy, Personal & Interpersonal development. It consists of Guest Lectures, Workshops, Field visits, Panel discussion, Group Discussion and many more.

Learn, Implement, Change.

Here is the info of some events:
• MUN (Model United Model): MUN is an extra-curricular activity in which students typically roleplay delegates to the United Nations and simulate UN committees. Students convene at Model UN conferences, to debate their assigned agendas/topics with other UN member states and nations’ delegates.
• Talk by Anil Swarup: Anil Swarup is an Indian author and retired Indian Administrative Service officer of Uttar Pradesh cadre and 1981 batch. He has served in various key positions like Education Secretary of India, Coal Secretary of India, Additional Secretary in the Cabinet Secretariat of India. As a strategic thinker and an innovative leader, he has won several awards and nominations. He will take a session on “Making Things Happen in the Government”.
• Shoot your Shot: For a film to have a real social impact, it has to be able to expand our understanding, to change our perspective, to challenge us to do something.
• Talk by Vijay Pratap Singh Aditya: First-generation technology entrepreneur, Vijay is the Co-founder, Director and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of ekgaon technologies Pvt. Ltd. He judged DBS-NUS Social Venture Challenge Asia, an Asia-wide competition for social enterprises. He will be sharing his views on “Growing Roots of India” and will ride us through his difficult and exciting journey of using IT to provide services to rural areas.
